# Suplex

## Overview

**[Suplex](https://trysuplex.com)** is an all-in-one cold email and lead generation platform designed for sales teams, agencies, and startups. It combines email automation, lead discovery, and CRM into a single platform.

## Key Features

- **Built-in Lead Generation**: Find prospects directly within the platform
- **Email Automation**: Multi-step sequences with smart scheduling
- **AI Personalization**: AI-powered email content personalization
- **Native CRM**: Built-in CRM for managing prospects
- **Multi-sender Rotation**: Better deliverability with sender rotation
- **Unified Inbox**: Manage all replies in one place
